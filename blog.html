BLOGS


ValueWalk

Started in 2010, ValueWalk.com offers breaking financial industry news — with a focus on hedge funds, large asset managers, and value investing. The site provides quality content that is important to value investors (most of which is free). It is read by senior level executives at the largest banks, hedge funds, asset managers, and Fortune 500 companies.





       


The Reformed Broker

This blog was started in November 2008 by the New York City-based financial advisor and CEO of Ritholtz Wealth Management, Joshua M. Brown.The blog covers markets, politics, economics, media, culture and finance. Brown uses “statistics, satire, anecdotes, pop culture references, sarcasm, fact, fantasy and any other device” to communicate his market-related insights.





       


Money Excel

This blog was started in August 2014 by financial advisor and blogger, Raviraj Parekh. It is a Personal Finance Website that helps you to make better financial decisions. The blog covers Financial Planning, Investment Planning and Retirement planning services for individuals in India and NRIs. it also provides fair guidance on fix income instruments, Equity, Mutual Funds, Insurance, gold and income tax.





       


FundsIndia

Started in January 2011 by its Directors CR Chandrasekar and Srikanth Meenaksh, FundsIndia is an online investment site and a investment blog headquartered in Chennai, Tamil Nadu.The website is owned by Wealth India Financial Services Pvt. Ltd.FundsIndia is India's friendliest online investment platform. It's goal is to empower and enrich Indian investors.It is very useful in building wealth by investing in mutual funds, stocks, and other investment.









       


SumZero

This site describes itself as “the world’s largest community exclusively for professional investors, providing quality, peer-reviewed investment research from top analysts and rising stars in the fund industry.” Members describe this site as network-enriching and career-enhancing.
